How to play ?

- This is a simple die game

- It has two player

- Click on the player name to change that

- Player 1's (Left side) turn starts first

- Click on the roll icon to start rolling the Die

- If either '6' or the previously rolled number turns up, then your turn will break

- Before the above mentioned condition comes, you can save the scores that you
have rolled by clicking 'GRAB SCORE' button

- Click 'GRAB SCORE' button will save your rolled scores to the total score section,
and breaks your turn

- The who reaches 50 points first is the winner

- You can also change the 'TARGET SCORE' by clicking on the same

- Key 'A' for 'PALYER 1' and 'L' for 'PALYER 2' can also be used to roll the die

- Press SPACE to HOLD / GRAB the rolled score
